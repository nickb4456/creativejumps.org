How It Works

01

Send a Photo

Email us a clear, well-lit photo of your pet. Front-facing or 3/4 angle works best.

02

We 3D Model It

We run your photo through Meshy AI, review the mesh, and slice it for our Bambu Lab A1 printer.

03

You Receive It

We print, inspect, package, and ship your figurine to your door. Turnaround: 3–5 business days.
